🖼️ Image Vectorizer

Upload a JPG or PNG drawing, restrict it to a small color palette, and convert it into a clean vector (SVG) image. Preview it before saving.

1. Image

📁 Click to choose an image, or drag one here

JPG or PNG — works best on drawings, logos, and flat-color illustrations.

2. Palette & Style

10

Fewer colors = simpler, bolder shapes. More colors = closer to the original image.

Controls how corners and curves are drawn.

8

Lower keeps tiny details; higher merges small speckles away.

0

Helps smooth out scan noise or JPG artifacts before tracing.

Higher = more detail but slower to process.

High detail + many colors + a large processing size can take a little while to trace — the button will show a status message while it works.
