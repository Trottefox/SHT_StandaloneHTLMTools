🔎 OCR — Image to Text

Load a photo or scan of a page, and get back editable, copyable text.

This tool runs OCR fully in your browser using the open-source Tesseract.js engine. The first time you use each language, your browser downloads that language's recognition data (a few MB) from a public CDN — this requires an internet connection once; after that it's cached and works offline. No image or text is ever sent to any server other than that one-time language-data download.

1. Image

Click to choose an image, drag one here, or paste (Ctrl+V) a screenshot.

JPG, PNG, WEBP, BMP… Higher resolution and good contrast give better results.
